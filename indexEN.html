Technological Innovation Fund

IoT Energy Monitoring System

During the last year I focused my efforts on the application of the technology readiness level methodology of NASA applied by CONACYT, to participate in July 2017 in the call of the Technological Innovation Fund.

The objective was to generate a commercial product, easily assembled and of easy installation to allow real-time consumption monitoring of electrical residential installations and display the information available in any kind of device, through Ahorratec.org

The development of this project had a bit of every discipline, from administration, countability, intellectual property, low and high-level programming, scientific research that I had to put my hands on as an entrepreneur and technical representative during the project

The call not coming out in 2017, forced me to end the project due to it being integral part of the business model to mature the technological development, I thank to my investors and personal that gave me their trust directly or indirectly, I will keep working on my spare time and probably will keep updating it with the latest technologies in a master’s degree.
